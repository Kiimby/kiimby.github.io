Submission for Con Latinidad in Celebration LATINX HERITAGE MONTH! in 2023.

I worked with college members doing environment illustrations and characters for this unity project.

## Gameplay
A farmer community is scared, a heat wave passed in Huehuetenango, Guatemala dried all the crops, now they don't have vegetables and fruits to consume and eat.

But there's hope, a charity organization was called to distribute provisions and is on it's way, but they will arrive in 5 days.

You control a family of that community, you must manage all day hours to make tasks, in order to the family to survive and don't stress out. Each member has special attributes, so be careful!

María, Anita, Juan and Josesito will thank you for your management!
